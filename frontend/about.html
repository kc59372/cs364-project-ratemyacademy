About

Rate My Academy is a platform created by cadets, for cadets, to provide a centralized and reliable way to share experiences about courses, professors, and academic workloads at the United States Air Force Academy. Instead of relying on scattered advice from friends or informal platforms, this system transforms those insights into organized, searchable reviews. Cadets can explore departments, evaluate instructors, and better understand course expectations through real feedback. By allowing verified users to read and submit reviews, Rate My Academy aims to support more informed decision-making during course selection and major planning while maintaining a simple, accessible web-based interface.
